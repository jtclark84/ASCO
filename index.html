  • ADAPTIVE MONITORING APPROACH

    The adaptive monitoring approach uses quality risk management as a foundation to ensure subject safety and data quality through the implementation of the following:

    • Building quality-by-design into trials.

    • Early and ongoing risk assessment.

    • Focus on critical processes and critical data.

    • Use of quality indicators and thresholds(via dashboards).

    • Adjustment of monitoring activities based on the issues and risks identified throughout the study (Risk assessment meetings).

  • Comparison:

    TRADITIONAL VS. ADAPTIVE TRIAL MANAGEMENT

    • TRADITIONAL
    • ADAPTIVE
    • COMPARE

    • Traditional

      • 100% SDV

      • Fixed site visit intervals

      • Standard approach for all studies

      • Responsibilities mostly limited to checking data

      • Focus on correcting errors, not preventing errors

      • Simple measures of performance

      • Create one plan, same actions for entire project

      • Focus on repeat activity

      • Handle issues as they come up

      • Standard guidance from performance goals or KPIs with standard corrective actions

    • Adaptive

      • Varying levels of SDV /SDR

      • Central oversight and management of information

      • There is no standard approach

      • Responsibilities include mentoring, coaching and site management

      • Focus on finding, correcting and preventing errors

      • Complex measures of performance using analytics and central management

      • Focus on predicting issues and prevention

      • Agile team management, quick and precise guidance based on results

  • Project Start up Adaptive trial management design

    Team creates the overall adaptive trial design or scheme based on AMAT

  • The trial scheme includes:

    • Frequency of analytic reviews.

    • Identification of protocol key risk indicators

    • Level of SDR

    • Level of SDV (Based on needs of that study and sponsor’s risk tolerance)

    • Type of central monitoring involvement

    • Monitoring Scheme and rules for escalation

    • Safety cuts for DSMB and/or DMC meetings

    • Interim analysis, blinded data reviews
